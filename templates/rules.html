rules

Be Respectful

No personal attacks or insults: Encourage kindness and constructive conversation.

No hate speech or discrimination: Make it clear that prejudice of any kind is not tolerated.

Respect opinions: Even when disagreeing, do so respectfully.

Keep it Clean

No profanity or offensive language: Set a standard for appropriate language.

No sexually explicit content: This includes text, images, or links.

No spamming or flooding: Discourage repetitive messages or excessive use of caps.

Protect Privacy

No sharing personal information: Remind users this is an anonymous space and to protect their own and others' privacy.

No doxing or revealing identities: Strictly prohibit any attempts to uncover someone's real-life identity.

Promote Good Communication

Stay on topic: While casual conversation is fine, discourage derailing the main flow.

No impersonation: Users should not pretend to be someone else.

Be mindful of others: Encourage users to be aware of the diverse community and avoid potentially insensitive topics.

Responsibility and Consequences

Users are responsible for their words: Clearly state that users are accountable for what they post.

Moderators have the final say: Explain that moderators can remove users or messages that violate the rules.
